<h3>Co-Curricular Activities (CCA)</h3>
<p></p>
<p>CCA ( Co-Curricular Activity) is an integral part of our students' holistic
education. Through CCAs, students discover their interests and talents.
CCA also provide platforms for students to develop their character, learn
values, social emotional competencies and other relevant skills to prepare
them for future chaallenges.</p>
<p>CCAs bring students from diverse backgrounds together. By learning and
interacting with one another, students develop friendships and deepen their
sense of belonging to the school and community.</p>
<p>Our CCA programme aims to enhance student development experiences by providing
a broad-based CCA programme for every student, through a two-tiered approach
designed to support students' holistic development.</p>

<p>The Tier 1 CCA Experience provides broad and foundational exposure, while
Tier 2 CCA Development offers speciaised and in-depth opportunities, to
nurture specific talents and abilities in identified students.</p>
<p>Through the CCA Experience and CCA Development programme students will
enjoy meaningful learning beyond the classroom . Students will begin their
CCA Experience journey from Primary 3 and all students are strongly encouraged
to participate in a CCA</p>
