<p>Dear Parents / Guardians,</p>
<p>I hope you and your family had a restful and meaningful term break, with
opportunities to spend quality time together.</p>
<p>As we begin Term 2, the school has planned a range of meaningful learning
experiences to engage and inspire our students, alongside holistic programmes
that support their academic, social and emotional development.</p>
<p>Selected students will participate in overseas learning journeys to Penang
(30 March – 2 April) and Da Nang (5 – 9 April) under our&nbsp;<strong><em>Trips for Internationalisation Experiences (TIE).</em></strong>&nbsp;These
experiences will help broaden their global awareness, deepen cultural understanding,
and build confidence and adaptability.</p>
<p>Students will also take part in&nbsp;<strong><em>Concord Experienza</em></strong>,
where they will engage in a range of activities to develop life skills,
strengthen problem-solving abilities, and broaden their horizons.</p>
<p>In addition, students will participate in the&nbsp;<strong><em>International Friendship Day (IFD)</em></strong>&nbsp;Commemoration,
where they will learn about Singapore’s connections with other countries
and the importance of cross-cultural understanding and cooperation through
classroom and interactive activities.</p>
<p>This term, we will continue to strengthen a safe, respectful and supportive
school environment, with a focus on the values of Responsibility and Integrity.
Students will be guided to make sound choices, be accountable for their
actions, and demonstrate respectful behaviour. We will also reinforce punctuality,
daily readiness, and the responsible use of digital devices to support
focused learning.</p>
<p>There will also be parent engagement sessions to strengthen our home-school
partnership and support your child’s learning journey.</p>
<p>Your continued support plays an important role in your child’s growth
and development. Together, let us nurture Concordians who embody&nbsp;<strong>Caring Hearts, Enriched Minds and Determined Spirits</strong>.</p>
<p>&nbsp;We look forward to working closely with you and your child this
term.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Yours truly</p>
<p>Mrs Quek-Chua Leng Yin</p>
<p>Principal</p>
